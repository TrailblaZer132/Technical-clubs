CP - Competitive Programming

The CITK Coding Club is all about imparting and assimilating Coding knowledge among the interested. We aim to conduct events related to the field and focuses on activities such as Competitive Programming, Coding Competitions, Seminars, Industry Talks, etc. We hope to bring the students who are passionately interested under one umbrella that is to our Club.



Club Aims

# To discuss programming concepts using various languages such as C/C++, JAVA, Python, etc.
# To increase the participation and performance of students at HackerRank, CodeChef and other coding platforms.
# To host various coding events in order to prepare for various coding competitions like Google Code Jam, ACM-ICPC, and others.

Our Team
