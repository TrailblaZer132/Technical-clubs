CITK DevCom

The CITK Developers Club focuses on the field of Web and App Development among the interested. We aim to conduct events related to the field and focuses on activities such as Web/App Development, Competitions, Seminars, Industry Talks, etc. The Club aims to contribute to the society on Web/App Development from the members with their skills and talent.



Club Aims

# To form a community of students having similar goals.
# To develop a minimum number of Mobile App/Web per year.
# To encourage members to contribute in Open Source Projects.
# To explore the demography of development such freelancing etc.
